Kadam

Visual and user experience strategy for a digital platform that empowers students to tackle sexual harassment at higher educational institutions

Overview

Kadam is a digital platform that provides tools and resources to empower students in higher education institutions to combat sexual harassment, assault, and abuse. It fosters a safer and more inclusive academic environment by enabling students to voice and act against such issues at their institutions.

My Role

As a UI and UX designer, I worked on the user experience and UI designs closely with the Executive Director, Director of Technology, Campaign Manager and developers to build the MVP.

My Process

Discover Define Ideate Deliver

The client has immense experience of raising awareness of college safety and sexual assault through various on-ground initiatives. I discovered their vision for the digital product that would increase awareness amongst students and arm them with knowledge.

Following the enactment of the Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH) law in 2013, governing bodies in higher education institutions have issued several policies and guidelines. However, empirical studies exploring the implementation and consequences of these initiatives have been scarce.

Discover Define Ideate Deliver

An interactive college database

There were a lot of data points to be shown for each college. To mitigate the issue of over information, we picked out the most relevant information and formed a vast table and filters around that information. Even when users would contribute to this database, we would only ask them for that limited information.

Community story map

To really show the users that they are not alone, we thought of creating the community stories on a map. Users would be able to visually see stories from around the country further enforcing this idea of there being other people from different locations who may be having similar experiences as them.

Discover Define Ideate Deliver

A warm and comforting space

An earthy colour palette was used for this platform to give the feeling of a warm and safe space. At the same time, a shade of orange and terracotta was used for the action buttons to impart a sense of action towards the CTAs,this platform is about empowerment after all.

A place of empowerment

The website uses language in a way of empowerment, encouraging users to take action and arm themselves with knowledge. Every page follows a narrative throughout, giving the information a logical flow and structure.
