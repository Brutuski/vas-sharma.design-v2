Overview

ACEplus is an e-learning app that focusses on 21st century skills through interactive courses, self-paced content and gamified learning.

My Role

As the Design Lead, I led the design team and worked in a cross-functional team to create the MVPs for the e-learning app, product e-commerce website, the content management system, the admin panel, employee dashboard and HR management dashboard.
I worked on the product strategy and execution, along with art direction for all multimedia graphic assets.
